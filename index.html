About Defy



Defy is a sports tournament for teenagers, by teenagers. We use our previous experiences with tournaments to ensure the best possible experience for all participants. All money raised from the tournament is donated straight to the Choice Foundation towards their initiative My Child Health and T.I.M.E.


The registration fee is 1000/-. This registration fee sponsors the comprehensive medical screening of one child.
